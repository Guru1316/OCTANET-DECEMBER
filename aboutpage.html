Introduction:


Welcome to Avalanche Kitchen, where the love for biryani knows no bounds! We're passionate about serving the finest, most authentic biryani experiences that will leave you craving for more.

Our Story:


Avalanche Kitchen was born out of a shared passion for biryani among friends. We experimented with various recipes, techniques, and flavors to perfect the art of biryani-making. Our journey took us to different parts of India, exploring regional specialties and secret recipes. Today, we're proud to bring our expertise to your table.

Our Mission:


To serve the most delicious, authentic biryani experiences that transport your taste buds to the heart of India.

Our Values:


1. Quality: We use only the freshest ingredients and traditional spices.
2. Authenticity: Our recipes are inspired by regional Indian cuisine.
3. Passion: We're dedicated to perfecting the art of biryani-making.
4. Customer Delight: Your satisfaction is our top priority.
